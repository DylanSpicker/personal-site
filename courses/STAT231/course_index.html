I am currently recruiting graduate students at both the Masters and PhD level.

I wanted to remind you all that if you have any questions regarding the content in the tutorials, you can always reach me by email (dylan.spicker@uwaterloo.ca) or on Piazza, and if you have any feedback at all, please feel free to reach out. Ultimately, it is my hope to try to use the tutorials to form some of “in-class” relationship that we are missing out on, being online, and if there is anyway I can improve that for you, please let me know! Thanks, and I hope you all have an excellent weekend and week!
